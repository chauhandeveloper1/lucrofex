How It Works

Three Steps.
One Is Yours.

Your entire role in this partnership is a single action. Everything else — forever — is handled completely by our team.

1

Contact Us — That Is Your Entire Job

Send us your name, your platform link (Telegram, Instagram, YouTube, or any other), and your approximate audience size. That is the full extent of what we need from you to get started. Our team personally reviews every submission — not a bot, not an auto-reply. We get back to you within 24 hours with a completely personalised plan tailored to your specific niche and audience profile. No commitment required at this stage. Just a conversation.

2

We Design and Build Your Branded Platform

Once we agree to partner, our team gets to work immediately. We design a fully custom application carrying your brand identity — your name, your colours, your content category. We develop it, test it, host it, configure monetisation, and make it ready to go live. You are not involved in this process at all. The average time from agreement to live platform is 24 to 48 hours.

3

Share It With Your Audience — Then Just Exist

When your platform is live, you share it with your followers — just as you would share any link in your posts, stories, or channel messages. That is the last active step required from you. Our monetisation system runs continuously in the background. Your admin panel updates in real time. Revenue accumulates. On a fixed monthly date, your share of the revenue is transferred to your account. Every month. Automatically.

∞

We Optimise. You Grow. Everyone Earns More.

The partnership does not end at step three. Our team continuously optimises your platform — improving ad performance, updating the app, refining the audience experience — because every improvement we make directly increases both our earnings and yours. You focus on growing your audience. We focus on maximising what that audience generates.
